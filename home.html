What is GameLink?

GameLink is an intuitive app designed for playing games and sharing your
creative works effortlessly with fellow users on Discord. Paired with our
dedicated bot, GameLink offers a seamless solution for aspiring creators
to showcase their talents, collaborate with others, and celebrate media in
all its forms, no matter the medium.

Born from years of research into various media production methods,
GameLink addresses a significant gap: the lack of platforms that allow
independent creators to showcase their work without facing gatekeeping.
We believe everyone should have the opportunity to explore their creativity
and share their passions without barriers.
